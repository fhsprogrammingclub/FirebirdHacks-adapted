Fremont High School's Second Annual Hackathon!

We bring together hundreds of high school students from across the Bay Area to work together and make their ideas a reality. We'll provide the food, refreshments and hacking spaces for you—the hackers—to do your magic. Just bring your ideas and your passion!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is a coding marathon event where people design and implement solutions to problems, or create new and creative softwares.

  • Can I work in a team?

    Competitors are allowed to work individually or in teams of up to 4 people.

  • What do I need to pay for?

    FirebirdHacks is completely free of charge! All food, drinks, and swag are included as we want to create the best possible environment for hackers.

  • What is Firebird Hacks?

    Firebird Hacks will be a fun way for hackers to get together and design innovative and useful solutions. Firebird Hacks, we will provide a learning environment for people of all experience levels to come and code!

  • What will I need to bring?

    You will need a laptop, a valid student ID (or some sort of student ID), chargers, your phone, a blanket/sleeping bag, toiletries, and any supplies you need for your hack.

  • Is experience needed?

    Not at all! In fact, Firebird hacks is a great way to begin your programming journey! A basic understanding is recommended, but we will have workshops and mentors to help you make your project idea a reality.
